ABOUT US

The Music Teacher Booking App is designed to connect music learners with skilled music teachers, making it easy to find, book, and manage lessons online. With separate modules for Admins, Music Teachers, and Clients, the platform ensures a seamless experience for both students and teachers, offering features like category management, class scheduling, payments, and feedback. Whether you're looking to learn or teach, our app provides a simple and efficient way to enhance your musical journey.
